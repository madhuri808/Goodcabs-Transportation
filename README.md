## 🎯 Objective

- 📊 Analyze dataset to address key business questions on:
  - **City-Level Trip Performance**
  - **Passenger Trends**
  - **Revenue Patterns**
  - **Target Achievement**
- 🖥️ Present findings in a **clear and visually engaging manner**
- 💼 Assist the **Chief of Operations** in making informed strategic decisions

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Overview

| Table | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `trips` | Individual trip records with city, fare, distance, ratings |
| `passengers` | New vs. repeat passenger breakdown |
| `cities` | City-level metadata and targets |
| `dim_date` | Date dimension with month and fiscal info |

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Areas

### 🏙️ 1. City-Level Trip Performance
- Total trips per city
- Average trip distance & fare
- City-wise revenue contribution

### 👥 2. Passenger Trends
- New vs. Repeat passenger ratio
- Passenger satisfaction ratings
- Monthly passenger growth

### 💰 3. Revenue Patterns
- Monthly revenue trends
- Revenue per trip by city
- Peak earning periods

### 🎯 4. Target Achievement
- Trips target vs. actual comparison
- Revenue target gap analysis
- Cities meeting/missing targets

---

## 💡 Key Insights

| # | Insight |
|---|---------|
| 🏆 | Top-performing cities consistently exceed trip & revenue targets |
| 🔁 | Repeat passenger rate is a key indicator of service quality |
| 📅 | Weekend & holiday seasons show highest trip volumes |
| 💸 | Longer trips generate higher revenue but reduce driver availability |
| 🎯 | 6 out of 10 cities meet monthly trip targets consistently |
| ⭐ | Cities with higher driver ratings show better repeat passenger rates |
